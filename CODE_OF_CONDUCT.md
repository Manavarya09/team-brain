# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contribute to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behaviour may be reported to **manav@manavaryasingh.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
